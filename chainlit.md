# 哲命 CheolMyeong — Two Lenses for Reading Yourself

Pick a **profile** at the top left to begin. *(한국어 안내는 브라우저 언어가 한국어면 자동 표시됩니다.)*

## 🔮 Saju Fortune
Type your **birth date, time, and gender** — e.g. `1998-11-11 22:00 male`
- Time optional (noon assumed); `19981111` works too. For lunar dates add `lunar` (leap month: `leap`).
- Your chart and a **Fortune at a Glance** appear instantly; category buttons give detailed readings —
  🎊 New Year · 🔮 Tojeong Bigyeol · 💕 Love · 💞 Compatibility · 💰 Wealth · 📜 Lifetime · 🌅 Today · 📅 Weekly · 🩺 Health
- Where **interpretation schools disagree** on the same chart, we show it rather than hide it.
- 🌐 To use Korean, press the **한국어** button in the welcome message or switch language in Settings (⚙️).

## 🧭 Philosophical Inquiry
Share **one sentence of what you believe** — e.g. `Love enriches life but also brings lack and pain`
- Your thought is split into core claims, similar claims are retrieved from a
  **3,600-node SEP knowledge graph**, and philosophers are ranked by **who actually asserts
  them** — with **source quotations** as evidence.
- Settings let you adjust top-N, claim splitting, and retrieval-only mode (skip the LLM).
- Log in to save diagnoses and find **kindred souls** (users whose philosopher
  distributions overlap yours).

> ⚠️ This service is for **fun and self-reflection**. Important decisions are always your own.
